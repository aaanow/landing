Our heritage

For over 25 years, we’ve been leading the way in website compliance (accessibility, carbon, experience and privacy) consistently developing innovative solutions.

1998

Research and development and initial test projects

2002

We launched the world’s first automated accessibility testing service. Making it easier for organisations to identify and fix website accessibility issues.
1st Gen

Panels

Early solutions focused on giving users more control over how they viewed websites and allowed for some personalisation of content.

2013

Our platform reached a significant milestone, analysing 1 trillion compliance datasets to identify potential accessibility issues and forecast ADA risks.

2017

We introduced a groundbreaking ADA Risk platform; enabling organisations to mitigate ADA risks, with embedded coverage.
2nd gen

Overlays (Widgets)

Marketed as simple AI-powered solutions — tools often fell short of promises and faced criticism for oversimplifying a complex issue.

2021

By analysing 3.5 trillion data points, our platform pinpointed content and technical accessibility needs. Market first in offering step-by-step supported, compliance guidance.
3rd Gen

Content Tools

Combine AI and human expertise to make websites accessible; reducing the effort by up to 90%, offering practical ways to achieve compliance.

2023

Introduction of AiCM; 3rd generation AI-powered tool that streamlines website accessibility.
